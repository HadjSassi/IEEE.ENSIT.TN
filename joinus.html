Join us on IEEE ENSIT Student Branch!
Are you interested in technology, innovation, and networking with like-minded individuals? If so, we invite you to become a part of our vibrant and dynamic community at the IEEE ENSIT Student Branch.
The IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is a prestigious global organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. The ENSIT Student Branch is an extension of this organization, specifically catering to students at the Higher National School of Engineers of Tunis.
The student branch aims to provide its members with opportunities for professional and personal development, networking, and collaboration.

ENSIT Student Branch, since 2013, embraces IEEE's values for innovation and humanity. 120+ members strong, we advance skills for a better engineering future.
