Chapter Overview

Our goal is to engage the worldwide technical community, especially underrepresented regions, in a full spectrum of professional development activities, including Publications, Conferences, Technical Panels, and Special Interest Groups. We have developed Distinguished Lectures and Short Courses to provide continuing education for our members' Chapters and Student Branches with either in-person visits to AESS Chapters or virtual presentations. About US

In 2023, the AESS IEEE Chapter was established at ENSIT Student Branch, focusing on Aerospace and Electronic Systems. It provides a platform for knowledge sharing, skill development, and industry networking. Through technical sessions, workshops, and conferences, members stay updated on advancements. The chapter fosters innovation, research, and community outreach, aiming to shape the future of technology and inspire engineering leaders.
